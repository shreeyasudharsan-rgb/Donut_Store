About The Store

As I grew up over the years, I knew what I had to do — my destiny. I had to make a random website for a school assignment... I mean, make an edgy donut shop to sell donuts consisting with the latest trends. Cutesy and goth at the same time. PNG backgrounds to exentuate the vides of emoness and scene. The darkness of my soul is what keeps me going during times of struggle with the buisness. join me in selling brainrot tralalero tralala donuts, and some very real radiant radical reckless red velvet donuts on our jouney to gothness and emoness.
